=== Titel
Extended DC_Table Driver


=== Suchbegriffe
api,driver,subpalette,palette,extended


=== Teaser
Allows the definition of subpalettes and offers a strong base for other driver developments.


=== Beschreibung
<h3>Features:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Supports definition of recursive subpalettes</li>
<li>Markup outsourced into templates</li>
<li>Cleaned up structure of loading and storing process</li>
<li>Good base for extension of the edit, editAll and overrideAll modes</li>
</ul>
